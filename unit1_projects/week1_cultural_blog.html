The value that I chose to expand upon is Inner Harmony. I think this is the most important value, because it is one of the most difficult. In the past, I thought inner harmony was achieved by remaining happy, but I now know differently. I used to do the things that kept me entertained, and chose to escape from my responsibilities and worries by having fun. When people saw me, they would think that I was carefree and fun loving, and I was, but I was not responsible. I learned about balance and keeping myself there, and I started to understand the path to inner harmony.
